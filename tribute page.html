JON BON JOVI

The man who gave love a bad name.


Here's a time line of Jon Bon Jovi:

  • 1975-Jon Bon Jovi began performing music live in 1975, at the age of 13, playing piano and guitar with his first band,Rave
  • 1985-In 1985, Bon Jovi's second album, 7800° Fahrenheit, was released. The band released three singles: "Only Lonely", "In and Out of Love" and the ballad "Silent Night"
  • 1992- Bon Jovi's fifth studio album Keep the Faith was released in November 1992, representing "the beginning of a new chapter in the history of Bon Jovi" and marking a change in the band's sound and image"
  • 2012-In January 2012, Jon Bon Jovi stated that they were working on a twelfth studio album,[52] and in a live show in August 2012, Jon Bon Jovi played an acoustic version of a song that was announced to be on the upcoming album.
  • 2009-In June 2009, Jon Bon Jovi and Richie Sambora were inducted into Songwriters Hall of Fame
  • Present-On March 9, 2019, Jon Bon Jovi announced via social media that the band is back in the studio recording its fifteenth studio album, to be released early 2020.[71] In August 2019, he announced that the new album will be called Bon Jovi: 2020
